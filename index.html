About Me

Hello! My name is Tülin Varol Mete and I have a PhD in high energy physics. During my PhD and postdoctoral fellowship, from 2010 to 2018, I was a member of the ATLAS experiment at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) at the European Organisation for Nuclear Research (CERN).

Officially, my job was to conduct data analyses to infer meaningful insights for new physics searches. It's actually a fancy way of saying to dredge up something interesting from the wreckage of two protons after they collide in a cavity that is as empty as interplanetary space and colder than outer space, literally in the coolest place in the universe!

Working as a physicist at CERN, I gained a wide-range of valuable experiences, including working with peta-byte scale data, utilizing cloud computing platforms, developing problem solving skills and implementing various statistical methods. I also served as a run coordinator for the ATLAS detector's Liquid Argon (LAr) calorimeter operation team, where I had to make time-critical decisions under high-pressure situations.

Currently, I am searching for exciting opportunities to challenge me to further improve myself while making contributions to high profile projects. I am very interested in machine learning techniques and trying to dive into that these days. I am using online platforms to my benefit to learn more about the subject. I am particularly amazed with Natural Language Processing (NLP) projects, where one can see algorithms that can even write poems! Isn't that amazing? It is very impressive to see that algorithms are becoming more and more capable of producing human-like outputs. Let me know if you have any suggestions/comments or want to team up for interesting projects.

When I don’t analyze data, you can find me dancing, cooking, trying new recipes, and learning French (oui, c’est moi :))

If you are interested, you can find links to my GitHub and LinkedIn profiles on the navigation bar up top, as well as a direct link to my CV. Feel free to get in touch with me. I would be happy to meet unique minds, exchange ideas, share my experiences and learn from your experiences.
